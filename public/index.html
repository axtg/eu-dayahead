About European Power Prices

This tool provides real-time European electricity day-ahead prices across 9 EU countries, helping consumers and businesses understand energy costs and optimize their usage.

Supported Countries

🇳🇱 Netherlands (21% VAT)
🇩🇪 Germany (19% VAT)
🇧🇪 Belgium (21% VAT)
🇫🇷 France (20% VAT)
🇦🇹 Austria (20% VAT)
🇨🇭 Switzerland (7.7% VAT)
🇩🇰 Denmark (25% VAT)
🇳🇴 Norway (25% VAT)
🇸🇪 Sweden (25% VAT)

How to Use

  • Country: Select your country to see local prices
  • Markup: Add your energy provider's markup (in euro cents per kWh)
  • Tax: VAT percentage - defaults to your country's standard rate
  • Timeframe: Choose between next 24 hours or today's prices
  • Copy URL: Get the API endpoint with your current settings

Data Sources

Prices are sourced from official European energy markets via the ENTSOE Transparency Platform and Stekker.app, updated hourly.

API Access

This tool is powered by a free REST API. Use the "Copy URL" button to get the endpoint for your current settings, or visit our GitHub repository for full documentation.
