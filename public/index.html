About EU day-ahead prices

This comprehensive tool provides real-time European electricity day-ahead prices across 9 EU countries, helping consumers, businesses, and energy traders understand electricity costs and optimize their energy usage. Track hourly electricity prices, compare energy costs across European markets, and calculate total costs including VAT and energy provider markups.

Supported European Energy Markets

🇳🇱 Netherlands - Dutch electricity market (21% VAT)
🇩🇪 Germany - German power exchange (19% VAT)
🇧🇪 Belgium - Belgian electricity prices (21% VAT)
🇫🇷 France - French energy market (20% VAT)
🇦🇹 Austria - Austrian power prices (20% VAT)
🇨🇭 Switzerland - Swiss electricity market (7.7% VAT)
🇩🇰 Denmark - Danish energy exchange (25% VAT)
🇳🇴 Norway - Norwegian power market (25% VAT)
🇸🇪 Sweden - Swedish electricity prices (25% VAT)

How to Use the Energy Price Calculator

  • Country Selection: Choose your European country to view local electricity prices and market data
  • Energy Provider Markup: Add your electricity supplier's markup cost (in euro cents per kWh) for accurate total pricing
  • VAT Tax Calculation: VAT percentage automatically defaults to your country's standard energy tax rate
  • Time Period: Compare prices for next 24 hours or view today's complete hourly electricity rates
  • API Integration: Copy the REST API endpoint URL for integration into your energy management systems

Official Energy Market Data Sources

All electricity prices are sourced from official European energy market data via the ENTSOE Transparency Platform (European Network of Transmission System Operators) and Stekker.app energy market API. Price data is updated hourly to ensure accuracy for energy trading, cost optimization, and consumption planning.

Free Energy Price API Access

This European electricity price tracker is powered by a comprehensive REST API service. Access real-time energy market data programmatically for energy management applications, smart home systems, and business energy optimization. Use the "API" button for complete documentation, rate limits, and integration examples. Visit our open-source GitHub repository for technical documentation and community contributions.

Base Endpoints

GET /api/{country}/today
Today's 24-hour prices
GET /api/{country}/next24h
Next 24 hours from now

Query Parameters

Parameter Type Description Example
markup decimal Fixed markup in EUR/kWh 0.024
vat decimal VAT as decimal (0.21 = 21%) 0.21
autoVat boolean Use country's default VAT true
roundTo integer Decimal places (default: 5) 3

Rate Limiting

  • Rate limit: 100 requests per minute per IP
  • Burst limit: 10 requests per second
  • Data updates: Hourly from official sources
  • Cache: Responses cached for 5 minutes
